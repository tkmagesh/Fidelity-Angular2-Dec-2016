    window.localStorage
    ===================
        * persistent
        * key/value store 
        * value has to be a string (use JSON.stringify & JSON.parse for serialization and deserialization)
        * APIs
        -----
            - setItem(key, value)   //Insert, Update
            - removeItem(key)       //Delete
            - getItem(key)          //Select
            - clear()               //Delete all
            - key(index)            //Key at index
            - length                //Count
